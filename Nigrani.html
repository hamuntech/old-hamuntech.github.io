Privacy Policy for Nigrani App

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Nigrani for mobile devices that was created by Mo Aslam at HamunTech. The Application is a device tracker that uses the geolocation coordinates of an Android device to determine the location. The user of the tracked device has to agree to be tracked by the owner of the tracking device by adding the tracking user as a contact in this app on their own device to provide their explicit consent.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

This app uses phone number authentication feature and geolocation coordinates as the core functionality of the app. Your phone number authentication and geolocation coordinates of your device are persisted on Google Firebase. We do not share this info with third parties or collect for any other purposes and the user has the ability to control the device location info and share the information with another user or entity.

Automatically Collected Information

This app does not collect, process, analyze or transmit any user or device related data to any other devices or entities, other than the phone number and geolocation persistence on the Google Firebase backend

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

The app uses your device’s geolocation GPS coordinates to determine the current location. This location is persisted on Google Firebase and is not transmitted to any other devices or entities by the developer or app development company. No location data is collected or persisted on any other devices other than as described here.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No third parties have any visibility to user’s application data.

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

User may retain application data on their device for as long as desired and any user data stored on the backend is not the property of any third parties, including this application’s developer or the app development company.

Children

We do not use the application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children of any age. No data is transmitted to any other devices and it persists only on the user’s device, other than as described in this policy.

Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. This application adheres to the built-in security features and policies of the mobile operating system vendor and does not knowingly exploit any system features or vulnerabilities.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.
