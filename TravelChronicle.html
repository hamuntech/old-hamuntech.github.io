Privacy Policy for TravelChronicle App

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application TravelChronicle for mobile devices that was created by Mo Aslam at HamunTech. The Application is a media organizer app that allows users to store the audio, video and pictures locally on the phone in reference to the device location, and retrieve and edit these assets later. It also allows users to share these files with others via the Apple IOS built-in sharing capabilities. This app includes a set of Image Filters as well that users can apply to pictures.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

All of the user provided information is stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to another device by any means, unless the user explicitly decides to share the information with another user or entity.

Automatically Collected Information 

This app does not collect, process, analyze or transmit any user or device related data to any other devices or entities.

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

The app uses your device’s geolocation GPS coordinates to determine the current location. This location is persisted locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to any other devices or entities. No location data is collected or persisted on any other devices other than the app user’s device. You have the ability to share your location with other people via built in iPhone sharing capabilities.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No third parties have any visibility to user’s application data. 

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

User may retain application data on their device for as long as desired and this data is not the property of any third parties, including this application’s developer or the app development company.

Children

We do not use the application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children of any age. No data is transmitted to any other devices and it persists only on the user’s device.

Security

We are concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. This application adheres to the built-in security features and policies of the mobile operating system vendor and does not knowingly exploit any system features or vulnerabilities.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to this Privacy Policy now and as amended by us.
